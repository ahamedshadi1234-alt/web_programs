Welcome to My Styled Inline Webpage

A fruit is the mature, seed-bearing ovary of a flowering plant, and can be either fleshy like a peach or dry like a nut. Botanically, the term includes many items not typically considered fruits in a culinary sense, such as tomatoes, cucumbers, and grains like wheat. They provide essential nutrients, fiber, and vitamins for humans and animals, and play a vital role in plant seed dispersal.
